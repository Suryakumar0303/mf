Privacy Policy

Your privacy is critically important to us. Learn how we handle your data.

1. Introduction

Your privacy is critically important to us at MyUndiyal. This Privacy Policy details how we collect, use, process, and protect your personal and financial information when you navigate our platform and utilize our services.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information to provide better services to our users. This includes explicitly volunteered data during risk profile analyses, contact inquiries, or account integrations, and automated generic analytical data.

3. Personal Information Collected

Personal information collected may include your name, email address, phone number, investment goals, risk tolerance, age, and any other KYC-associated details required for mutual fund registration purposes.

4. How We Use Your Information

The information collected is used strictly to understand your financial needs, provide personalized investment recommendations, respond to your queries, and map out strategic goal-based planning for mutual funds.

5. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

MyUndiyal uses cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance user experience, track website performance, and maintain session integrity. You can control cookie preferences directly through your browser settings.

6. Sharing of Information

MyUndiyal does not sell, trade, or rent users' personal identification information to others. We share your KYC and investment data exclusively with Asset Management Companies (AMCs) and trusted platforms like AssetPlus as legally required to execute your investments.

7. Data Security

We adopt industry-standard data collection, storage, and processing practices. We take appropriate physical, digital, and administrative security measures to protect against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction of your personal information.

8. Data Retention

We retain personal information only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which we collected it, including for the purposes of satisfying any legal, regulatory, tax, accounting or reporting requirements strictly mandated by SEBI or AMFI.

9. User Rights & Choices

You have the right to request access to the personal data we hold about you. You may ask to correct inaccurate data, update your contact preferences, or request data deletion where appropriate under current governing Indian laws.

10. Contact Information

For any questions or concerns specifically regarding this Privacy Policy, your rights, or our data handling practices, please connect with us securely via email at myundiyal@gmail.com.
