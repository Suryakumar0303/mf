What is a Risk Profile?

A risk profile determines your capacity and willingness to endure financial risk. It is influenced by your age, financial goals, time horizon, and personal comfort with market volatility.

Knowing your risk profile is an essential first step before making any investment. We categorize investors into three simple profiles:

Conservative Investor

Prioritizes capital protection over high growth.

Moderate Investor

Seeks a balance between risk and reward.

Aggressive Investor

Wants maximum wealth creation and accepts high volatility.
